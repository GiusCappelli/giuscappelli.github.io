About Me

I'm a Physicist with a love for building things and sharing knowledge. This site serves as both a portfolio of my work and a place where I practice writing through regular blog posts. English is not my first language, and I haven't yet written enough to consider myself a proficient writer (proof: what I've just written). I believe that writing helps clarify thinking, and I'm excited to share my thoughts on my research, as well as technology, development, and whatever else catches my interest.

When I'm not working on my thesis, you can find me learning about Linux, system control, programming, game development, or strumming the mandolin. At this point in time I am very scattered. I'm always eager to learn new technologies and improve my craft in research, development and writing.
